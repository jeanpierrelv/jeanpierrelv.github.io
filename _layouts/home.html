Research

I am a researcher at the Prisma Research Group, Department of Statistics, Applied Mathematics and Computing, São Paulo State University. My research focuses on computer vision with deep learning, algorithms for morphometric measurements from 3D tomographic images, and large language models for AI solutions.

Main areas: Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Time Series Forecasting, Information Retrieval, Large Language Models, Control Systems, Robotics.
