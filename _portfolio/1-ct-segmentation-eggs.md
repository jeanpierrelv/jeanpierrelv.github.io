## CT Image Segmentation for Chicken Eggs

Non-destructive morphometric measurement extraction from 3D computed tomography images using deep learning segmentation.

**Technologies:** Python, TensorFlow, Keras, 3D Deep Learning (U-Net, FCN)

**Key Results:**
- 98.69% accuracy for egg component segmentation
- Automatic extraction of height, width, shell thickness, and volume
- Non-invasive alternative to traditional destructive methods

**Related Publications:**
- Alternative Non-Destructive Approach for Estimating Morphometric Measurements of Chicken Eggs from Tomographic Images with Computer Vision (Foods Journal, 2024)
- Generation of Simulated Dataset of Computed Tomography Images of Eggs and Extraction of Measurements Using Deep Learning (ESANN Conference, 2024)
