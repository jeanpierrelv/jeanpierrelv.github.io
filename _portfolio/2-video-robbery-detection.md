## Video Robbery Detection with Self-Supervised Learning

Self-supervised learning approach for video surveillance that leverages human pose information to detect robbery events in static camera footage.

**Technologies:** Python, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Pose Estimation, Self-Supervised Learning

**Key Contributions:**
- Three novel self-supervised pretext tasks incorporating human pose information
- Human-centric approach replacing pixel-based methods
- Insights into pose-based self-supervised learning for surveillance applications
