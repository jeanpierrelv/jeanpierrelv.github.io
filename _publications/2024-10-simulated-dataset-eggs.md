This paper extracts morphometric measurements of the different volumes of chicken egg components (shell, yolk, albumen and air chamber) by evaluating the segmentation algorithms, U-Net and Fully Convolutional Network (FCN). It also presents a new dataset of 3D CT images of chicken eggs in DICOM format with labeled masks.

The 3D models trained end-to-end showed high generalization even in the presence of variations in egg size and internal structures, achieving state-of-the-art segmentation performance with 99.4% accuracy.
